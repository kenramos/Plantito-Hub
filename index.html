Welcome Plant Lovers

Want to start taking care of plants but don't know where to start? Or tried having plants but end up killing them? Or maybe irritated at the ballooning prices of plants being sold out there?

You've come to the right place.

What is in store here?

Browse through the plants and see tips and details on how to take care of them. Read articles on personal experiences of plantitos, newbies and veterans. Post your own plants for sharing.

Who we are

We are plantito newbies and plant lovers who have created a platform for sharing knowledge and even baby plants to those who wish to partake in the relaxing world of indoor gardening.

Let's collaborate

Be part of the community. Share your experiences in taking care of your own plants. Help newbies start their plantito journey by sharing your baby plants (or even mature ones), seedlings, cuttings and other gardening stuff for adoption.
